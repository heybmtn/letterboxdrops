Common questions

Everything you need to know.

What areas in Oxford do you cover?

We cover the whole of Oxford and the wider Oxfordshire area, including Cowley, Headington, Summertown, Jericho, Botley, Abingdon, Witney, Didcot, Banbury, and beyond. If you have a specific postcode or neighbourhood in mind, just let us know and we'll confirm coverage.

How much does leaflet delivery in Oxford cost?

Distribution starts from £60 per 1,000 leaflets, with a minimum order of 5,000. The total cost of your campaign will depend on the volume, area, and whether you need printing. We'll provide a clear, all-in quote with no hidden fees before any work begins.

Do I need to supply my own leaflets?

Not at all — we offer a complete print-and-distribute service. Simply supply your artwork (or tell us if you'd like help with design) and we'll handle everything. If you already have printed stock, we're happy to distribute your existing leaflets too.

How will I know the leaflets have been delivered?

We provide a delivery report on completion of every campaign. You'll receive this directly to your inbox so you have full peace of mind that your leaflets reached the right doors.

How quickly can you turn around a campaign?

Timescales depend on campaign size and print requirements. Once you submit your quote request, one of our team will get back to you within one business day with a full timeline. For urgent campaigns, just let us know and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Can I target specific streets or types of households?

Yes. We can target by postcode sector, specific streets, or demographic profile — so your leaflets reach the households most likely to become your customers. Just mention your preferences when requesting a quote.
